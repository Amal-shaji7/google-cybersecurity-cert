# Security Audit Report – Botium Toys

## 1. Overview
This report presents the findings of a security audit conducted on Botium Toys’ internal security controls, systems, and compliance posture. The objective of the audit was to identify security gaps, assess risk exposure, and recommend improvements aligned with industry standards such as PCI DSS, GDPR, and SOC frameworks.

---

## 2. Scope and Objectives

### Scope
The audit covers the entire security program of Botium Toys, including:
- Employee devices and internal systems
- Network infrastructure
- Data storage and retention practices
- Legacy systems
- Physical and environmental security controls

### Objectives
- Evaluate existing security controls
- Identify vulnerabilities and compliance gaps
- Assess risk levels
- Recommend actionable improvements to strengthen security posture

---

## 3. Risk Assessment Summary

- **Overall Risk Score:** 8 / 10 (High)
- **Key Risk Drivers:**
  - Lack of access control mechanisms
  - Absence of encryption for sensitive data
  - No backup or disaster recovery strategy
  - Weak password policies
  - Limited monitoring and detection capabilities

---

## 4. Key Findings

### 🔴 High Risk Issues
- Lack of least privilege access control
- No encryption for sensitive customer and financial data
- Absence of backup and disaster recovery plans
- Weak password policies
- No intrusion detection system (IDS)

### 🟠 Medium Risk Issues
- No separation of duties
- Lack of password management system
- Inconsistent monitoring of legacy systems
- No data classification framework

### 🟢 Low Risk Observations
- Firewall properly configured
- Antivirus protection in place
- Physical security controls (locks, CCTV, fire systems) are adequate

---

## 5. Business Impact

The identified vulnerabilities expose Botium Toys to:

- **Data Breach Risk:** Sensitive customer and financial data could be compromised
- **Regulatory Non-Compliance:** Violations of PCI DSS and GDPR requirements
- **Operational Disruption:** Lack of backups may lead to data loss and downtime
- **Unauthorized Access:** Weak access controls increase insider and external threat risk

---

## 6. Recommendations

### Immediate Actions (High Priority)
- Implement encryption for sensitive data
- Enforce role-based access control (RBAC)
- Establish secure backup and disaster recovery processes
- Strengthen password policies

### Short-Term Improvements
- Deploy an Intrusion Detection System (IDS)
- Implement a centralized password management system
- Enforce separation of duties

### Long-Term Improvements
- Implement data classification policies
- Standardize legacy system monitoring procedures
- Conduct regular security audits and control reviews

---

## 7. Conclusion

The audit identified several critical security gaps that significantly increase the organisation’s risk exposure. Immediate action is required, particularly in access control, data protection, and monitoring capabilities.

Addressing these issues will improve Botium Toys’ overall security posture, reduce the likelihood of data breaches, and ensure better compliance with industry regulations.
