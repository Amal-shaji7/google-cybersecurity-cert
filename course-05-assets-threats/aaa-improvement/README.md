# Access Control Investigation — Authentication and Authorization

## Overview

A growing business discovered an unauthorised payroll 
transaction to an unknown bank account. This activity 
involved reviewing an event log to investigate the 
incident, identifying the threat actor by cross-
referencing the employee directory, spotting access 
control failures that enabled the incident, and 
recommending mitigations to prevent recurrence.

The investigation revealed that a former contractor's 
administrator account remained active four years after 
their contract ended — and was used to access payroll 
systems and initiate a fraudulent bank transfer.
